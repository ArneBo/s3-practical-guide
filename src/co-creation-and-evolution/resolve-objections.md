## A way for resolving objections

![right,fit](img/agreements/resolve-objections-process.png)

* resolve objections: 
    * hear one objection
    * amend proposal 
    * ask for any objections to amendment
    * resolve objections to amendment (hear/amend/ask/resolve)
    * process next objection
